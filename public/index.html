About Sadri Villa

Sadri Villa is a heritage of the Sadri family in Shahpur Baghauni village of Tajpur block in Samastipur district of Bihar.

This historical building is nearly 150 years old. The ancient owner of this villa was a freedom fighter and a doctor. The monument is architecturally beautiful and includes a small park that visitors can explore free of cost.

Sadri Villa provides temporary night shelter for helpless people. The family also allows economically weak people to arrange marriage ceremonies free of cost. More than 100+ marriages have been successfully conducted here.
