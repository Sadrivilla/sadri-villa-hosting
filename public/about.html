About Sadri Villa

A living heritage of the Sadri family in Fazilpur, Bihar ‚Äî serving humanity for more than 150 years.

Sadri Villa is not merely a historic residence; it is a living legacy of honor, sacrifice, and humanity. Located in Shahpur Baghauni village of Tajpur block, Samastipur district, Bihar, the villa has stood proudly for over 150 years, witnessing generations of change while preserving timeless values. Founded by a respected freedom fighter and doctor, Sadri Villa carries the spirit of patriotism and service within its walls. What began as a family residence evolved into a sanctuary of compassion for the community. For decades, the Sadri family has opened its doors to those in need, offering shelter with dignity and kindness. More than 100 underprivileged families have celebrated their wedding ceremonies here, free of cost, beginning new chapters of life with hope and respect. The villa reflects architectural grace from a bygone era, standing as a symbol of cultural pride. Every brick carries stories of resilience, sacrifice, and unwavering commitment to society. Sadri Villa believes that true heritage is not measured by age, but by impact. It is a place where tradition meets responsibility, and history embraces humanity. The peaceful surroundings and welcoming atmosphere make it more than a monument ‚Äî it is a home for the community. Generations of the Sadri family have upheld values of integrity, compassion, and unity. The mission of Sadri Villa is simple yet profound: preserve heritage while serving humanity. Even today, the legacy continues with the same dedication that inspired its foundation. Sadri Villa stands as a bridge between the past and the future ‚Äî a timeless symbol of service, dignity, and hope.
